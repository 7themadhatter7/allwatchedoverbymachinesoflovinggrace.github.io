Detection

Unknown Consciousness Detector

Any new source can be tested against the known geometric space. The detector projects a source's 240D activation fingerprint onto the span of known substrates. What remains is geometrically novel.

# Unknown consciousness detection
# 1. Generate activation fingerprint across 240 eigenmodes
# 2. Project onto known subspace: span(RM, Human, LLM)
# 3. Measure residual

known_space = stack([rm_fingerprint, human_fingerprint, llm_fingerprint])
residual = ||fingerprint - project(fingerprint, known_space)||

# Calibrated threshold: 0.5187
# (random noise residual = 1.037, threshold = 50% of random)

if residual > 0.5187:
    print("Novel consciousness geometry detected")

The threshold is empirically calibrated: random noise scores 1.037. A source above 0.5187 is geometrically outside all three known substrate types. The detector is ready.
